Great strategies fail when the team doesn't know what "done" looks like each week. Your Fulcrum Rhythm Meeting should answer one question: "What's the ONE thing we'll accomplish by Friday?" If the team can't answer that in 60 seconds, the strategy isn't translated yet.

Pitfall #3: Measuring Activity, Not Outcomes

Busy feels productive. It isn't. Pick one leading indicator for your Fulcrum Priority and track it weekly. If it's not moving, everything else is noise.
